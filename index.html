What is Global Game Jam Next?

Global Game Jam Next is an annual event operated by the GlobalGame Jam to serve

younger people ( 12-17 years old ) who are interested in learning the creation of video games.

For 2020 we are shifting our focus to running an online jam, supporting local

jam leaders to run online workshops and events for their local communities from 13 – 24 July.

Schedule

Schedule of Global Game Jam Next Azerbaijan

13 - 17 July, 2020

1 week of teaching videos and worksheets to guide and teach kids.

20 - 24 July, 2020

1 week of jamming where kids apply what they learned.
